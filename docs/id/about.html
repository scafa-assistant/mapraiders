Mission

Social connection belongs to the street, not the feed

The big social networks have a fundamental problem: they optimize for attention, not for connection. Their business model is advertising, their tool is the algorithm, and their result is isolation behind glowing screens.

MapRaiders takes the opposite approach. The social network IS the map itself. What you see is not curated — it is real. Who is active in your city, who holds territories, who leaves echos, who creates quests: all of this happens outside, in the world, visible to everyone who is also outside.

No profile picture is recommended to you because an algorithm wants to maximize engagement. You encounter other players because you are literally in the same place. This kind of connection cannot be faked — and that is exactly the point.

Origin Story

Founded in 2025 — with one simple question

What if the map were the social network? Not a map with social features bolted on, but a platform where geographic position is the foundation of every interaction?

MapRaiders was launched in 2025 under Scafa Investments LLC (Cutler Bay, Florida, USA). The impulse: existing location games like Ingress or Pokémon GO show that people are willing to walk around the real world for digital content. But none of them took the next step — treating the map as the primary social medium.

The team started with one core idea: territories as personal statements. When you hold a zone in your city, that is a statement. It is visible. It demands real presence. And in that lies the social signal that no algorithm can manufacture.

Technology

Built for real GPS performance

Mobile — React Native / Expo

The MapRaiders app is built with React Native on Expo SDK. TypeScript throughout, Zustand for state management, on-device GPS tracking with a privacy-first design. No location data leaves the device without explicit user action.

Server — Node.js + PostgreSQL + PostGIS

The backend stack runs on Node.js with Express and TypeScript. PostgreSQL 16 with the PostGIS extension enables high-performance spatial queries — polygon overlaps, proximity checks, and territory merge operations in real time.

Real-Time — WebSocket + Redis

All live events (territory attacks, echo detections, event updates) flow through WebSocket connections. Redis handles session management, rate limiting, and pub/sub between server instances.

Privacy First — no tracking business model

MapRaiders does not collect data for advertising networks. GPS data is used exclusively for game mechanics. No third-party tracking, no data resale, no advertising SDK in the app.

Our Values

What MapRaiders will never be

No algorithm

What you see on the map is unfiltered reality. No recommendation engine decides which content you see — only geography and the activity of real people.

No ads — ever

MapRaiders will never introduce an ad-funded model. Future revenue comes exclusively from optional creator tips and premium features that enhance gameplay — not manipulate it.

User Ownership of Content

Quests, echos, challenges, and territories belong to the players who created them. MapRaiders is a platform, not a content owner. Your creations are your creations.
