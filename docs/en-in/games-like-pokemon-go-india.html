The problem

The Pokémon GO problem in India 2026

Four structural pain points define why Indian players look elsewhere:

  • Pay-to-Win drift, priced in dollars. Remote-Raid passes, battle pass, monthly token bundles, all priced for US wallets. ₹500 to ₹1000 a month on seasonal content that disappears does not match Indian ARPU.
  • AR battery drain on Tier-2 phones. Augmented Reality eats batteries alive, and a Tier-2 budget device feels it about twice as hard. On a long walk the game dies inside an hour.
  • Tier-2 and Tier-3 devices treated as an afterthought. Most GPS games target flagships, while budget Androids with 2 to 3GB of RAM are a side concern. India has more than 600 million such devices in active use.
  • Saudi acquisition in March 2025, plus the Niantic geospatial-AI angle. Indian players' walks fed Niantic's training data, and that company now sits inside Scopely, owned by the Saudi PIF. With DPDPA in force, the trust picture got more complicated.

FAQ

Common questions

Which game like Pokémon GO is actually free in India?
MapRaiders is the only indie option in the top seven that keeps the full gameplay free; cosmetic-only IAP starts at ₹19 and is UPI-friendly. Geocaching has a free tier but locks most of the good experience behind premium, and Walkr's free tier is similarly thin.
Are there games like Pokémon GO that aren't owned by Niantic?
Yes. Since the March 2025 Niantic-Saudi deal, all four Niantic location-based games (Pokémon GO, Ingress, Pikmin Bloom, Monster Hunter Now) are owned by Scopely, a Saudi PIF subsidiary. MapRaiders is the major non-Niantic option, a privately held US LLC with DPDPA-aligned data handling.
Are there games like Pokémon GO without AR for Tier-2 phones?
MapRaiders is intentionally AR-free. GPS plus map, nothing more. Result: roughly four times the battery life on a long route, and stable performance on 2GB RAM budget Androids. Tu jaana hai, bas walk karo, the phone will keep up.
Which alternative has cricket-fan features?
MapRaiders' cricket-fan map app feature is India-exclusive. Claim your IPL stadium zone, your team's neighbourhood, your match-day territory. None of the other GPS games in this list have anything like it.
Are there games like Pokémon GO that work in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities?
MapRaiders works wherever GPS works: Indore, Lucknow, Coimbatore, Bhubaneswar, Patna and so on. There is no PokéStop-style dense-network requirement; you can claim territory anywhere with an active GPS signal.
