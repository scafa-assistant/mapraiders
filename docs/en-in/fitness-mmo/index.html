Fitness Apps Track. MapRaiders Fights Back.

You've downloaded the running apps. You've set step-count goals. You've watched your streak break on day 11 because walking for data just gets old. Fitness tracking isn't broken — it's just missing stakes.

The problem is nothing is on the line. Your 10,000 steps today don't stop someone else from gaining ground. Your consistency doesn't build anything that persists beyond a graph.

MapRaiders changes the equation. Your movement isn't data — it's conquest. Every block you walk is ground you can own. And someone else wants it too.

The Exercise Game GPS Built Around Territorial Competition

MapRaiders is a massively multiplayer game where the only way to play is to physically move through the real world. Your GPS trace becomes your territory. The city map is shared by everyone.

🏃

Movement = Progress

Walk, run, or cycle. Every meter of real-world movement earns territory on the MapRaiders map. No passive progress. No shortcuts. Movement is the only currency.

🗺️

Live Competitive Map

The map updates in real time. See your territory grow, see rivals claim adjacent zones, see the frontlines shift. The fitness motivation that actually lasts.

⏳

Decay Keeps You Honest

Territory weakens if you skip days. The decay system replaces streak pressure with something better: real consequences on a shared map. Others gain what you abandon.

👥

Clan Teamwork

Form a crew with friends or coworkers. Hold territory as a team. When one member can't go out, another covers the ground. Real group accountability.

How the Fitness MMO Loop Works

  • 1
    Move — any way you like Walk, run, or cycle. Every GPS coordinate your body visits translates directly into in-game territory. No screen tapping required during your workout.
  • 2
    Claim territory and build your map As you cover new ground, your territory expands on the live map. Compete with others in your neighbourhood for zone dominance.
  • 3
    Keep moving to defend what you own Territory decays if you stop visiting. Regular workouts translate directly into a stronger map position. The game rewards consistency, not intensity.
