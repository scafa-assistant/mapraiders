What makes a territory game tangible

The territory test runs along three experience axes:

  • Conquest. When does the first claimed street feel like “my land”?
  • Loss. How do you react to the first decay or losing land to an attacker?
  • Defence. How do the defence mini-games feel — tactical, fair, frustrating?

The three testers' quotes cover all three axes from very different perspectives — the Indian player can map the same experience to their own neighbourhood.
