// Core Systems

Built for the Streets

Six core systems that transform every sidewalk into a battlefield and every neighborhood into an empire.

Territory Claiming

Walk through real streets to claim hexagonal zones. Build resource structures, defend your borders, and expand your empire block by block.

Quests & Echos

Discover location-bound quests and ghostly echoes left by other players. Every corner holds a story, every alley hides a challenge.

Duels & Races

Challenge nearby raiders to GPS-tracked races or tactical duels. Speed, strategy, and street knowledge determine the winner.

Clan Wars

Form alliances, build clan headquarters, and wage coordinated wars for district control. Strength in numbers, strategy in movement.

Night Layer

When the sun sets, the map transforms. New zones appear, shadow mechanics activate, and rare resources emerge only in darkness.

Weather Events

Real weather data triggers in-game events. Rain floods spawn water zones, storms unlock lightning mechanics, snow reveals hidden paths.

// The Loop

How It Works

Four steps. One game loop. Infinite territory.

🚶

Walk

Move through the real world. Your GPS tracks every step, every route, every shortcut.

⚑

Claim

Step into unclaimed hexes to capture them. Build structures. Generate resources passively.

⚙

Create

Place quests, set traps, leave echoes. Shape the world for everyone who walks after you.

⚔

Compete

Duel raiders. Race rivals. Siege clan forts. Rise through the leaderboard by walking harder.
