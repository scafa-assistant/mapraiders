Why now

Why “Niantic alternative” became a 2026 search trend

Three signals converged between 2024 and 2026:

  • #HearUsNiantic. The largest organized player backlash in LBG history, running from 2023 into 2025. Remote Raid Pass price hike, Avatar overhaul, Wizards Unite shutdown. Players got loud, organized, and stayed loud.
  • The geospatial-AI confession. Niantic's CEO admitted publicly that “users were essentially providing free training data for our geospatial AI model”. The framing “Pokémon GO was a scam” spread fast on Reddit.
  • The Saudi acquisition in March 2025. Niantic sold its game division to Scopely under the Saudi PIF for $3.5 billion. Two trust shocks compounded into one search trend: “Niantic alternative”.

FAQ

Common questions

Is MapRaiders affiliated with Niantic?
No. MapRaiders is independent. Built by Scafa Investments LLC, no relationship with Niantic, Scopely or the Saudi Public Investment Fund.
Is MapRaiders affected by the Niantic-Saudi deal?
No. The March 2025 Niantic deal sold Pokémon GO, Ingress, Pikmin Bloom and Monster Hunter Now to Scopely. MapRaiders has zero connection to any of that.
Will MapRaiders accept investment from sovereign funds?
No. MapRaiders is privately owned and is committed to staying independent. No state-controlled investment, period.
What's #HearUsNiantic about?
A player movement that ran from 2023 into 2025, protesting Niantic's monetization decisions. Remote Raid Pass price hike, Avatar overhaul, Wizards Unite shutdown. It's the largest organized player backlash in LBG history.
How does MapRaiders avoid Niantic's mistakes?
Cosmetic-only IAP. Ad-free. AR-free GPS gameplay. Persistent territory ownership instead of fleeting captures. Organic clans instead of factions. A public commitment against data-selling and state ownership.
