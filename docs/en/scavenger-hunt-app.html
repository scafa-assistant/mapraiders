What counts

What a modern scavenger hunt app needs

Three things separate 2020s scavenger-hunt apps from the print-and-clue era:

  • Live. Clues exist in real time, not only inside pre-built tours.
  • Social. Players leave clues for each other instead of grinding through static stations.
  • No premium gate. Parents and kids start playing instantly, without buying a $4.99 tour first.

How we rethink it

How MapRaiders rethinks the scavenger hunt

Instead of a linear tour from station one to station ten, MapRaiders creates an open spatial scavenger hunt where the city itself is the playground:

  • Drop Echoes. Leave an audio, photo or video Echo at a real location. Other players find it when they walk past.
  • Find Echoes. See on the map where Echoes are. Follow the trail, find secrets, hear stories.
  • Build quests. Create a small task at a place (“photograph the red door across the street”). Other players complete it.
  • Territory layer. Walk a scavenger route often enough and it becomes claimed territory. Your hunt becomes your land.
Family use case

Scavenger hunt app for families

Scavenger hunts have always been a family thing. MapRaiders brings that into the smartphone era without sticking kids alone in front of a screen:

  • Parent-and-kid activity. Parents drop audio Echoes along a planned route. Kids follow the clues. Physical movement, digital hints.
  • Screen-light by design. The app shows the map. The experience happens in the real world.
  • COPPA-aligned. No personally identifiable information from minors, no ads, parental mode available for restricted gameplay.
FAQ

Common questions

Is MapRaiders safe for kids?
Yes, ages nine and up with parental supervision. The app collects no personally identifiable information from minors (COPPA-aligned), is ad-free, and has a parental mode for restricted gameplay.
How much prep do I need for a scavenger hunt with kids?
Zero. Unlike Actionbound or Goosechase you don't buy a tour or pre-set stations. Echoes are already scattered across the city. You follow other players' clues or leave your own.
Are there scavenger hunt apps for adults?
Yes. MapRaiders' Echo system and clan-territory mechanics scale from family fun to competitive multiplayer.
Does it cost anything?
No. The scavenger-hunt features (drop Echoes, find Echoes, build quests) are fully free. Cosmetic items ($1.99 to $9.99) are optional and give zero gameplay advantage.
Does it work in small towns?
Yes. Even in small towns or rural areas you can drop Echoes and build quests. Dense cities have more clues from other players. Rural areas leave your own route more room for solo exploration.
