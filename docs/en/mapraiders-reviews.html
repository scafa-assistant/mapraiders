5.0 out of 5 from three closed-beta reviews

The reviews come from the closed beta between February and April 2026. Ron tested in Stuttgart, Vivian in Hamburg, Aljoscha in Berlin. All three used the game on their own routes, not in some artificial test setting. The originals are written in German; the English versions you see here are translations, marked in Schema.org via translationOfWork so it stays transparent.

From the founder
I played Pokémon GO for three years and eventually quit. The thing I was missing never showed up: real land instead of fleeting gym captures. When the Saudi acquisition hit in 2025 it was clear to me that the Niantic model wasn't heading anywhere I wanted to follow. So I'm building MapRaiders myself. No ads, no investor pressure, no required sub. My block is my playing field; yours is up for grabs.
René Scafarti
Founder, Scafa Investments LLC
