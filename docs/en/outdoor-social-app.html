The Problem

What's Missing from Outdoor Social Today?

Fitness apps track you. Social media addicts you. Neither actually connects people in the real world. Strava gives you stats, but you're alone. Instagram shows you a highlight reel, but you're sitting on the couch watching it. The two categories co-exist but never converge.

What's missing is an app where physical presence has social consequences. Not "like my running photo" — but: your run claims territory. Your route leaves traces other players interact with. Your neighborhood becomes a shared arena. MapRaiders is built exactly on this premise: outdoor movement as the engine of real social connection.

The Solution

How MapRaiders Reinvents Outdoor Social

GPS Territories

Walk through any street and claim it. Your territory appears live on the map for all other players to see. The more you move, the more you own.

Echos — Audio Graffiti

Record a voice message, a music clip, or a challenge at any GPS location. Other players discover it when they walk past. Asynchronous social connection anchored to place.

Organic Clans

Clans form naturally — play with friends, ally with neighbors, build territory together. No bureaucratic join requests or forced team assignments. Real community.

Quests & Challenges

Create quests for other players at real locations. Set challenges that require physical presence. Your creativity shapes the outdoor experience for your entire neighborhood.

FAQ

Quick Answers

What is an outdoor social app?
An outdoor social app combines physical movement with social interaction. MapRaiders is the strongest example: you go outside, claim GPS territories, leave audio Echos for other players to discover, and build organic clans. Social media meets outdoor adventure — without the toxic parts.
How is MapRaiders different from Strava?
Strava tracks your performance data. MapRaiders turns your movement into social gameplay. Your walk doesn't just log stats — it claims territory, triggers battles with neighbors, and creates location content other players discover. Movement has actual social consequences.
Do I need special gear?
No. Any Android smartphone with GPS is enough. MapRaiders works for walking, jogging, or cycling. Every form of outdoor movement earns territory and contributes to your presence on the map.
Is MapRaiders free?
Yes, 100% free. No pay-to-win, no premium barriers, no battle pass. All players compete on equal footing. Success comes from movement and strategy, not spending money.
When does MapRaiders launch?
MapRaiders is in final development and launching soon on Google Play. Sign up for launch notifications now to be among the first to claim territory in your city.
